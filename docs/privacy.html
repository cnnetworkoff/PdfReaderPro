Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-14

PDF Reader Pro is maintained by cnnetworkoff. This page applies to the Play Store distribution of the app package com.pdfreader.cn.

Data Collection

PDF Reader Pro does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking systems.

Files and Local Data

The app accesses PDF files you choose to open or browse on your device. Bookmarks, favorites, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. Passwords for protected PDFs, if you choose to save them, remain stored locally using encrypted storage.

Network Access

This Play Store build does not download or install APK updates from inside the app. Updates are delivered through Google Play. Network access may still be used for opening web links or optional online/network PDF access paths supported by the app.

Third Parties

PDF Reader Pro is open source software and includes third-party libraries under their respective licenses. License notices are available in the app and repository.
